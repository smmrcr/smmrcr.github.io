Sam Mercer is an artist and researcher living and working in London. He works as a producer of the Digital Programme at The Photographers' GalleryHe completed an MRes Art: Moving Image course at Central St. Martins with a focus on artists' broadcasting and use of television formats. Sam often works collaboratively, including as part of Common Study, Bad Vibes Club, Aas and Tether.
