Experimental Film Generator

Configure a template and download the full 3249-frame image. The film will play at 24fps giving a length of around 2m21s.

Each frame is 160x120px | 57 Columns x 57 Rows

Controls

  • R : Repeat/Reset animation to frame 1
  • C : Choose and load your film
  • S : Toggle Optical Sound
  • F : Toggle Full Screen
  • D : Record and Download as Movie (.webm)
  • G : Open this Generator page
